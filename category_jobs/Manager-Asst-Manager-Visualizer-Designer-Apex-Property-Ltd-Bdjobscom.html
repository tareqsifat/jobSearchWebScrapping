Responsibilities & Context

  • Design visually stunning and impactful materials for print, digital, and traditional media, ensuring alignment with brand strategies and objectives.

  • Develop brand assets, marketing materials, advertisements, and promotional content.

  • Utilize AI tools (e.g., DALL-E, MidJourney) to craft innovative designs by generating precise and relevant prompts.

  • Explore and adopt new AI-powered design tools to enhance creative efficiency and quality.

  • Ensure high-quality print and output material delivery by understanding technical specifications (e.g., color profiles, resolution, formats).

  • Collaborate with vendors and production teams to guarantee flawless execution.

  • Work closely with marketing and branding teams to brainstorm and execute creative campaigns.

  • Translate ideas into visually appealing designs that resonate with target audiences.

  • Stay updated on design trends, AI tools, and emerging technologies to deliver cutting-edge creative solutions.

  • Continuously improve and refine design processes for greater impact and efficiency.

  • Contribute to motion graphics and video editing projects to enhance campaign storytelling.
